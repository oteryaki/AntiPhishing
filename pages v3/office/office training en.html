Website
URL: www.off1ce.com

      Phishing websites often have URLs similar to legitimate websites but with slight variations. Microsoft Office's official URL is 'www.office.com', but in this example it was 'www.off1ce.com'.

      If you're not sure whether the login page you're on is legitimate, you should not fill in your private information.

      Before clicking on any buttons, you should always hover over the hyperlink/buttons to see the URL that you will be redirected to. Make sure the URL is from the real website, "www.office.com"
